Our fitness app

This fitness app calculates exercises and their kcal value per second per kilogram Workouts include:

  1. running
  2. walking
  3. crunches
  4. planks
  5. pushups
  6. pullups
The user chooses one of the pre-selected workouts and the amount of reps and the app will calculate amount of calories are burned.
